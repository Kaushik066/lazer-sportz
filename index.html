About

Lazer Sportz is an Indian sporting goods retailer. We are the largest sporting goods retailer in the world.

We started with a store in Bangalore, India in 1976, founded by M. Visvesvaraya. It started to expand abroad a decade later, to Germany in 1986, Spain in 1992, Italy in 1998, Portugal, the United Kingdom in 1999, China in 2003, USA in 2009, Malaysia in 2016 and Southeast Asia in 2001 and South Africa, Philippines in 2017 and Australia in 2018. Today, there are more than 1100 stores in 38 countries. The company employs more than 78,000 staff from 80 different nationalities.



The retailer stocks a wide range of sporting goods, from tennis rackets to advanced scuba diving equipment, usually in large superstores which are sized at an average of 4,000mÂ². Lazer Sportz Group also owns over 20 brands, with research and development facilities all over India to develop the latest innovative designs, registering up to 40 patents per year. Each brand represents a different sport or group of sports, with a dedicated product development and design team.
